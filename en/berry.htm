MARINE CLUB Berry

Marine Club Berry is the main water sports center in Okinawa since year 2001 that operate whole year round. Due to weather concern Marine Club Berry have 3 branches that cover east and west side of Okinawa, with the location coverage there is no worry about the weather or sea condition on your holiday plan for water sports with Marine Club Berry. Marine Club Berry offer numbers of water sports activity in Okinawa.

Leisure Diving

Marine Club Berry organizes leisure diving around Manza and Nakama area in Okinawa. The diving site is unique from the seascape with beautiful nudibranch, shrimp or school of fusilier, sea turtle, and grouper. Popular dive site Cape, Onna Point, Honsyu, Manzamou Shita, Manza Coral, Over Head Rock, Manza Dream Hole, Mini Dream Hole, Seragaki Out leaf, Nakayukui, Cross Line, Nakama Point, Afro and Bitaro.
